Cookie Policy

Learn how we use cookies to enhance your learning experience

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and allow us to improve our services.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

Analytics Cookies

We use these cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our content and user experience.

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and settings to provide you with a personalized experience on our platform.

How We Use Cookies

At CodeMaster, we use cookies for various purposes:

  • To remember your login status and preferences
  • To track your learning progress and course completion
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • To provide personalized course recommendations
  • To improve our platform's performance and security

Cookie Management

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our cookie practices.
