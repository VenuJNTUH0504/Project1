Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start learning on CodeMaster?
To start learning on CodeMaster, create an account, choose your learning path, and begin with our introductory courses. We recommend starting with the fundamentals before moving to more advanced topics.
What programming languages are available?
We offer courses in various programming languages including Python, JavaScript, Java, C++, and more. Each language has multiple courses ranging from beginner to advanced levels.
How do I track my progress?
Your progress is automatically tracked in your dashboard. You can view completed courses, current progress, and upcoming lessons. Certificates are awarded upon course completion.
